## Product result

R3.7 consumes the frozen R3.5/R3.6 architecture and adds reversible local
product depth:

- shared non-secret preferences resolve workspace and frontend defaults;
- managed instances can be inspected, verified, compared, cloned, renamed,
  archived, restored under a new identity, and moved to reversible trash;
- deterministic portable snapshots carry hash closure, exclude volatile and
  secret content, compare against live instances, restore without source-path
  leakage, and use reversible retention;
- declarative templates and launch profiles support create, clone, inspect,
  diff, plan, apply, archive, safe arguments, and existing-state compatibility;
- local mod inventory covers managed roots and built-ins with stable archive,
  dependency, hash, validity, and reference information;
- local modset planning is deterministic, bounded, cycle-safe, budgeted, and
  transactionally applies and rolls back exact state;
- save intelligence structurally indexes and associates up to 10,000 saves,
  detects drift, and applies reversible no-substitution retention;
- server configuration planning validates, diffs, and exports secret-free
  headless bundles without opening a socket or starting a process;
- TUI guided forms replace raw-JSON entry for generated operational commands;
- WinForms and AppKit consume the same generated field and visualization
  metadata, with WinForms client runtime proof and AppKit compile proof;
- the complete lifecycle composes across direct FLB, bounded stdio, CLI, TUI
  direct/guided/process, and the generated WinForms command client.
