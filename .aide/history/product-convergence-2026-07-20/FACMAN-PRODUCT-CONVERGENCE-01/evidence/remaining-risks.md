# Remaining Risks

- Real Play remains unavailable. The capability-scoped process supervisor,
  versioned run session, recovery model, and Launch module belong to
  `FACMAN-EXECUTION-FOUNDATION-01`.
- Instance-isolated Steam-aware Play and hermetic standalone Play remain
  independent, unproven gates. Automated evidence cannot record their human
  verdicts.
- The desktop experience remains command-centric; the world-first first-run
  journey and user pilots belong to playable alpha.
- M3 existing-portable adoption is preserved as authorised post-alpha backlog;
  adoption apply and existing-install mutation remain unavailable.
- AppKit runtime, publisher authenticity, signing, notarisation, publication,
  network, credentials, server processes, daemon work, and AI remain
  unavailable or deferred.
- The source revision in a dirty local build stamp identifies the configured
  Git commit, not a claim that uncommitted files were published or authentic.
