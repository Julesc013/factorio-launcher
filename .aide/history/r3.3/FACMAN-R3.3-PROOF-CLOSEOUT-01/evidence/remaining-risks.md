# Remaining Risks

- Real Factorio isolation remains a human-gated unproven claim.
- `run.execute`, Safe beta, setup mutation, networking, credentials, dynamic
  plugins, server execution, publisher authenticity, signing, and publication
  remain unavailable or unpromoted.
- Shared/network filesystem guarantees remain unpromoted pending separate
  hostile-lock and multi-host evidence.
- The Linux package claim is limited to its exact Ubuntu 24.04 x64/glibc 2.39
  runner and recorded dependency set; the Windows ZIP is local and unsigned.
