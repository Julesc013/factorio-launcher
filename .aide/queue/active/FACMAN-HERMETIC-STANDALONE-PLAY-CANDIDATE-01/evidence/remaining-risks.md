# Gate 4B remaining risks

- No real Factorio process has run through the candidate.
- The normal Factorio menu, absence of an inferred save, save creation/load,
  clean exit, last-run report, and relaunch persistence have not been observed
  by a human reviewer.
- The independent Windows process-tree filesystem/registry capture backend and
  real-host protected-root comparison remain unproven against Factorio.
- The accepted source-evidence packet is candidate-scoped and is not a general
  authenticated install, repair, or update provider.
- The technical packet can say only `eligible_for_human_verdict`; its human
  verdict remains unset.
- Product permit issuance, public `instance.play`, real execution authority,
  Setup, credentials, network, Steam, alternate intents, signing, publication,
  playability, and Safe beta remain unavailable.
- The Gate 4B task-owned temporary root remains retained until final integration
  evidence is preserved or an approved exact-root deletion mechanism succeeds.
